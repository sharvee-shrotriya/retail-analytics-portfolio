Procurement Intelligence: Key Findings
Recommended Actions for Leadership
1
China vendor negotiation is a capped strategy. Beijing's active involvement makes cost absorption demands unsustainable. Vietnam/India diversification must accelerate beyond the current ~4% share.
2
The 4,000-SKU model is a genuine structural advantage. Sourcing pivots that take Walmart 18+ months take Costco 6–9. Procurement should prioritize SKU-level tariff triage immediately.
3
Kirkland domestic shift to 70% is the right playbook. Extend this model to hardlines and select electronics categories where domestic or nearshore alternatives exist.
4
Membership fee buffer is real but finite. At 65% of net profit from fees, Costco can absorb ~120–150bp of margin compression before member value proposition is at risk. Current exposure is 3.8%.
5
Supplier risk AI is the most underpenetrated lever. Only 21% of retailers use AI for supplier monitoring today. Early investment here directly reduces the next tariff shock's impact on procurement.
