# The Kirkland Moat
### Costco Supply Chain and Private Label Strategy Analysis

## What This Is

A supply chain and competitive strategy analysis examining why Kirkland Signature's local sourcing model is a permanent structural advantage — not just a tariff hedge.

The conventional read is that Costco shifted Kirkland sourcing to dodge tariffs. This analysis argues that's too narrow. Local sourcing reduces procurement costs, accelerates replenishment cycles, and lowers stockout risk regardless of the tariff environment. Costco wins whether tariffs are at 145%, 15%, or 0%.

---

## The Research Question

Does Kirkland Signature's local sourcing model create durable competitive advantage beyond tariff avoidance?

---

## What the Dashboard Covers

**01 — The Trigger**
Costco Q3 FY2025 earnings miss, 7% stock drop, CEO's sourcing announcement, and the full tariff policy timeline from March 2025 through March 2026.

**02 — The Moat**
How local sourcing creates structural unit economics advantages: 70% domestic manufacturing, 30–40% COGS savings, SKU curation enabling faster supply chain pivots.

**03 — Head to Head**
Kirkland Signature vs. Member's Mark (Sam's Club) across seven dimensions: sourcing model, SKU count, tariff exposure, membership fee buffer, renewal rate, replenishment speed, and strategic framing.

**04 — Scenario Analysis**
Costco's competitive position stress-tested at 145%, 15%, and 0% tariff rates. The moat holds in all three scenarios.

**05 — Competitive Context**
China sourcing exposure across Walmart, Target, and Costco with peer comparison.

**06 — So What**
Strategic implications for Costco, Sam's Club, and supply chain analysts.

---

## Key Finding

The 10-K says it plainly: higher tariffs hurt Costco. But the strategic response — local sourcing, supplier consolidation, SKU discipline — positions Costco better than any peer regardless of the tariff outcome. Membership fee income at 65% of net profit means the core business model doesn't depend on merchandise margins surviving a policy shock.
