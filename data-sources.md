### Methodology Notes
- Import origin mix percentages (~58% China, ~18% domestic, ~12% Mexico, ~8% Canada, ~4% other) are estimates triangulated from Census Bureau trade data, 10-K disclosures, and analyst reports. Costco does not publicly disclose exact sourcing breakdown by origin.
- Margin impact figures are illustrative models, not financial projections. Built using BLS import price index movements and COST segment gross margin disclosures.
- Scenario modeling assumptions available on request.
