## Data Limitations

- Member's Mark import mix is not separately disclosed by Sam's Club or Walmart at the private label level. Competitive exposure figures are estimated from parent company disclosures and third-party research.
- Costco has not disclosed the exact dollar amount of IEEPA tariffs paid. The ~1/3 imported sales figure is derived from CFO commentary, not a line item in financial statements.
- The 70% domestic Kirkland figure and 30–40% COGS savings are sourced from earnings call commentary, not audited financial statements.
- Tariff scenario percentages (145%, 15%, 0%) reflect historical peak, current Section 122 rate, and a hypothetical resolution scenario respectively.
